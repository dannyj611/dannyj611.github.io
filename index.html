About the RMU Bands

When you join the bands and performance ensembles at Robert Morris University, you make a unique and special connection to RMU. Our groups perform for a multitude of school, community, and national events. These include RMU athletic events both at home and throughout the United States, Pittsburgh area parades, and nationally recognized dance competitions.

Our students demonstrate their excellence in both academics as well as musicianship. Members of our groups truly have a passion for performance and put on an incredible show everywhere they go.
